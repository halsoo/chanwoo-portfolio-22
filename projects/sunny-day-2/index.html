Imagining and depicting sustainable tommorrow and future of technology with XR and Wearable.

Brief

CUBE is an interface and interaction design project that researched what kind of interaction people can do in the moving medium - mobility(cars) - when the fourth stage of autonomous driving is commercialized in the future.

Context

With Auto driving 4.0, People will have more spare time in vehicles in the future. To imagine various scenarios and design an interface for them is needed.

Solution

Came up with Multi-modal, Surface shifting Interface and Seat
